"WHAT'S SIGN"



We’ve all been asked this question — whether on a date, by an acquaintance or that frenemy. Whether or not you’ve ever read in-depth about the zodiac signs doesn’t matter because I can guarantee you know your zodiac sign. You wouldn’t be reading this article if you weren’t at least a little bit curious about astrology.

As a basic definition: Astrology is an art form that uses the placement of the planets and luminaries to give insight into aspects of one’s psychology, as well as make life predictions.

Some back-story (drum roll, please): Since the birth of mankind, we have always looked to the stars. By our very natures, we are curious animals — eager to better understand the world around us. Not only have we sought to create our imprint as a civilization on the Earth, but we have sought to understand our place in the cosmos and what our purpose here is.

The zodiac is composed of 12 different signs — each with its own personality traits, strengths, weaknesses and characteristics. So if you’ve ever wondered. “Why are you like that?” about someone — this is a big part of the reason why.

As a little disclaimer, your Sun sign is tied to your exact day of birth and it is what most people are referencing when they ask about your zodiac sign. This ties to your core personality and psychological profile — hinting at what is important to you throughout your lifetime.

However, the Sun sign is just one aspect in your birth chart that helps illuminate what makes you entirely unique. In fact, you have many other placements that are also deeply insightful: your Rising Sign/Ascendant, Moon Sign and beyond. After all, astrology teaches us that you are cosmic because you have all the stars inside of you.




ARIES




Element: Fire

Quality: Cardinal

Power Color: Red

Ruling Planet: Mars

Symbol: Ram



Bold, pioneering and courageous, Aries is the leader of the zodiac. They are daring and adventurous and are unafraid to strike out into unknown territory where others would be unable to go. An eternal child at heart, Aries are energetic and aggressive, filled with an enthusiasm for life. They can be quick to anger, but also just as eager to laugh and move on. These people tend to be ambitious, active and honest, eager to open themselves up to every one of life’s possibilities. Aries are independent and enjoy sharing their fiery personalities with the world.
